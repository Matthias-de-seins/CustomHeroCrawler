Inai, the Void Spirit, is a melee intelligence hero that relies on the element of surprise to blast apart foes with the power of the void. He plays like no other hero, relying on a wide variety of skills that allow him to put his enemies out of position while quickly jumping in and out of combat. Using his Aether Remnants, Inai can start and end any fight in seconds by forcing enemies to attack remnants of himself that guard from all angles. The void is both sword and shield for Void Spirit, whether he takes a quick repose in the aether to teleport to an area of his choice with explosive force, or shields himself in volatile energy that slays the weak and nullifies the attacks of the mighty. Should anything manage to survive the Void Spirit's onslaught, he calls upon his mastery of the ultimate Astral Step technique, dashing through space and time to attack and mark enemies for death not once, but twice.

Bio[edit]

Inai, the Void Spirit
▶️ "Friend or foe, in the wake of the confluence, you will follow where I lead."
Lore:
Not even the other spirits claim to understand the mind and machinations of the eldest amongst them, Inai the Void Spirit. Privy to secrets that would shatter a mortal mind, the Void Spirit observes the workings of the universe from an unknowable vantage, choosing to let carefully groomed servants do his bidding -- only emerging from his Hidden Temple into the material plane when he deems that he alone can steer the course of reality along its proper path.

With an expansive perspective on existence, Inai is focused intently on a point in time beyond which he cannot see, when the fabrics of multiple realities are fated to collide. Now he steps from the aether to personally resolve the battle of the Ancients and prepare his allies for what he views as a greater conflict to come.
Voice:
Rene Mujica (Responses)

    • Sets the target's movement speed to 44%/50%/56%/62% of the distance between Remnant and the target.
    • If the target is too close, it stops moving once within 100 range of the remnant.
  • The speed is set once upon cast. If the target gets moved or teleported, the speed does not adapt.
  • Aether Remnant first applies the damage, then the pull debuff.
  • The level 15 talent adds 600 radius True Sight around the remnant's location.

Notes:

  • Creates 7 portals, with one of the being at the cast location.
    • The remaining 6 portals form a ring around the center portal.
    • One portal is created in front of Void Spirit, while the other 5 are adapted to it, with 60 degree angles between them, forming a hexagon.
    • All portals are placed 520 range away from the center of the middle portal.
  • The player can decide at which portal to re-appear, by issuing a move order. A move order is given by right-clicking, or by pressing the move hotkey and selecting a target point.
    • Directly clicking on the portals is not required, a directional order is enough. The closest portal to the right-clicked location gets selected.
    • The player can change the selected portals as many time as they want during the phase duration.
    • A selected portal is highlighted for Void Spirit and his allies. For enemies, all portals look the same.
  • Turns the caster invulnerable and hidden for its duration.
  • Fully disables, silences, mutes, and disarms Void Spirit, making him unable to do anything.
    • Attack orders behave like move orders during disarms, so right-clicking an enemy selects the closest portal to the enemy as well.
  • Does not provide phased movement, so Void Spirit can still block other units during Dissimilate.
  • Does not disjoint projectiles upon teleporting.
  • The damage is dealt around the selected portal upon re-appearing.
    • The level 25 talent also adds a stun to this. Applies the damage first, then the stun.

  • Blocks after armor reduction, but before any other reductions.

Recommended items[edit]

Starting items:

  • Tango keeps Void Spirit alive in lane.
  • Enchanted Mango helps him spam his abilities on enemy heroes in laning phase.
  • Healing Salve is essential for any hero in the laning phase.
  • Quelling Blade helps him obtain last hits more easily in lane.

Early game:

  • Ring of Basilius keeps Void Spirit's mana pool high and builds into Veil of Discord.
  • Phase Boots help Void Spirit chase enemies down and make him even more resilient against attacks.
  • Bottle is useful for sustaining Void Spirit in the mid lane and storing runes.

Mid game:

  • Kaya gives Void Spirit increased attack and spell damage, along with reduced mana costs to sustain his spellcasting.
  • Veil of Discord augments Void Spirit's area of effect spell damage, letting him nuke down enemy teams faster.
  • Maelstrom is a great item for farming and pushing and also increases your damage in fights with Astral Step.
  • Orchid Malevolence grants you a silence and damage amplification to use with your abilities on a single target, also granting large amounts of mana regen to spam your abilities. This can be easily upgraded later into a Bloodthorn if you have the gold.

Late game:

  • Black King Bar is a good defensive item for him as he can be shut down with silences and magical bursts.
  • Linken's Sphere saves Void Spirit from immediate, single targeted disables (like Scythe of Vyse) that can lead him to death before he can activate Black King Bar.
  • Bloodstone is a great option to upgrade your Kaya. Not only is it item-slot efficient, but it also allows you to spam your spells even more, as well as give you even more spell damage.
  • Shiva's Guard is a good item for you and your team. The armor synergises with Resonant Pulse (armor is calculated before the shield HP). It also gives great stats and you can spread the slow to everyone on the enemy team with your high mobility.
  • Abyssal Blade is a good item as you not only gain a damage block, but a guaranteed stun which could be followed up with a Dissimilate for escape or damage. Your Astral Step further works with this item by potentially proccing the passive bash.

Situational items:

  • Radiance continues to do damage while Void Spirit is using Dissimilate. The damage emanates from Void Spirit's original position before using Dissimilate.
  • Octarine Core raises health and mana for sustainability. The Cooldown Reduction and Spell Lifesteal allows Void Spirit to keep himself healthy.
  • Scythe of Vyse grants attributes, intelligence, and mana regeneration to let Void Spirit use more abilities. The active Hex disables a dangerous target and sets up Dissimilate.
  • Desolator improves the damage of Astral Step, which can also spread the armor debuff to multiple enemies.
  • Eul's Scepter of Divinity is a good choice against mobile targets or heroes with silences that can shut you down. Not only does it provide an escape against these heroes, but it can also be used as a setup for Aether Remnant.
  • Item needed.


Gameplay[edit]

Roles: Carry Escape Nuker Disabler
Complexity: ★★☆
Playstyle: From the Hidden Temple, Inai steps into the material world to perform tasks that change the trajectory of the world. Dissimilating and reappearing in random portals, the Void Spirit leaves behind glowing Aether Remnants that pull enemies into their destructive embrace. Sending out a Resonant Pulse, he shrouds himself in the essence of the void, absorbing incoming attacks. A tear in reality is observed as the Void Spirit Astral Steps through his enemies, leaving a trail of ruin in his path.

Trivia[edit]

  • Void Spirit's Chinese voice actor is Ge Ping (葛平), who also worked on 3000 Whys of Blue Cat, a famous cartoon series in China.
  • Void Spirit was first teased in a comic that came with the release of Monkey King's arcana, almost 3 years before his official release.
  • Inai is the negation of the Japanese verb いる (iru) which means "to exist".
